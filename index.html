faq

How long does it take to build a website? +

Most projects are delivered in 2–4 weeks from kickoff. The timeline depends on scope and how quickly you can provide content (copy, images, brand assets). We move fast — and we'll let you know exactly what we need upfront.

How much does a website cost? +

Most small business sites start at $1,500–$3,500. E-commerce and custom web apps vary based on complexity. We give clear, flat-rate quotes before any work begins — no surprises.

Do I need to provide content and copy? +

You can — but you don't have to. We offer full copywriting and can source or optimize photography. Just tell us what you have and we'll handle the rest.

Will my site work on phones and tablets? +

Always. Every site we build is mobile-first and tested across devices and browsers. With 60%+ of web traffic on mobile, this isn't optional — it's the baseline.

What happens after the site goes live? +

We offer ongoing support packages for updates, performance monitoring, and new features. Or we hand it off cleanly so you or your team can manage it. Either way, you won't be left stranded.

Do you work with businesses outside the US? +

Yes. We work with clients anywhere in the world. All communication is async-friendly and we accommodate different time zones without issue.
